Who are Fashion Man?

We started in 2006 and haven't stopped smashing it since. A global brand that does not sleep, we're 24/7 and always bringing something new with over 100 new products dropping on the daily, bringing you the latest looks for less.

Fashion Man recognizes individuality. We see you. We embrace it. We make fashion accessible and fun. Fashion Man is for girls. All girls.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Our philosophy's pretty simple: we don't take fashion or life too seriously.

From over 4 million fans on social and an online magazine, we're the dress you need at the weekend, and the moments you need when you're down.

OUR CHARITY STRATEGY

We all know that it's better to give than to receive, that's why we have set up Fashion Man's charity programmer to raise money and awareness for causes close to our hearts.
